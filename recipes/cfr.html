Chicken Fried Rice

A Takeaway classic, made at home.

This recipe couldnt be simpler!

  1. Find a Chicken
  2. Send chicken to culinary school, nothing fancy, Just so he can use a hob.
  3. Build some steps in your home so the chicken can reach the oven.
  4. Provide the chicken with a Wok and some Rice
  5. Have the chicken cook the rice for you.

And there we have it, plate up and enjoy!
